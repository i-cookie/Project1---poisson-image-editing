step 1. set source and target images

replace the source and target images ('src.png' and 'tgt.png') in the ./Components folder.

Step 2. Run the 'Poisson Image Editing.py'

Step 3. Press and hold left mouse button to draw the ROI, you can redraw if you are not satisfied. Only the last ROI will be kept.

Step 4. Press space to confirm ROI

Step 5. Set the target region by clicking on the target image, you will see the region preview.

Step 6. Press space to confirm and wait for the result. The intermediate images like mask and the result are saved.
